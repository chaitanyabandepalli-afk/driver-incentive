# Project Objectives

## Project Title
Driver Incentive & Performance Bonus Tracker

## Company
Manivtha Tours & Travels

## Objectives

1. To create a digital system that allows admin or staff users to enter driver performance details such as trips completed, punctuality, customer rating, and complaints.

2. To reduce manual work by replacing spreadsheets, WhatsApp messages, and paper-based tracking with a centralized dashboard.

3. To automatically calculate monthly driver incentive amounts based on predefined business rules and performance KPIs.

4. To provide a dashboard where management can view, search, filter, and monitor driver performance records.

5. To improve transparency between management and drivers by maintaining accurate performance and payment records.

6. To identify top-performing drivers based on punctuality, customer ratings, and number of completed trips.

7. To provide reports and analytics that help management understand monthly performance trends and incentive payouts.

8. To build a complete working prototype with frontend screens, backend APIs, database storage, testing documentation, GitHub repository, and deployment.
