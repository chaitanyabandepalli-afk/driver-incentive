## Work Completed Today

Today I worked on the Day 2 tasks of the internship project. I prepared the problem statement and abstract for the Driver Incentive & Performance Bonus Tracker. I understood that the main issue faced by Manivtha Tours & Travels is the manual tracking of driver KPIs such as punctuality, customer rating, trips completed, and monthly incentive payout.

I described how manual tracking through spreadsheets, WhatsApp messages, phone calls, or paper records can create errors, delays, confusion, and lack of transparency. I also wrote the project abstract explaining how the system will provide a digital platform for entering driver performance data, calculating incentives automatically, showing records in a dashboard, and generating reports.

Since I am working individually, I also prepared the frontend screen list and backend business rules. I identified the important modules such as Admin Dashboard, Driver Performance Entry Form, Driver Incentive Dashboard, Detail and History View, Reports and Analytics Dashboard, and Automated Calculation Engine.
