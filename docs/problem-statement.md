# Problem Statement

Manivtha Tours & Travels is a transportation and car rental company that manages multiple drivers for airport transfers, outstation trips, corporate travel, wedding travel, and long-distance tours. The company needs to track driver performance regularly based on important factors such as punctuality, customer rating, trips completed, and monthly incentive payout.

Currently, if this work is handled manually through spreadsheets, WhatsApp messages, phone calls, or paper records, it can lead to errors, delay, confusion, and lack of transparency. Manual calculation of driver incentives may also create disputes between drivers and management because there is no centralized system to verify performance data and payout rules.

The company requires a digital system that can store driver performance records, calculate incentive amounts automatically, display all records in a dashboard, and help management identify top-performing drivers. This system will reduce manual work, improve accuracy, motivate drivers to perform better, and support fair incentive distribution.
